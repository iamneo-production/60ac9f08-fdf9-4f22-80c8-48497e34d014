Welcome to Empower Her

Empower Her is a mentorship program connecting girls with STEM professionals.

Our goal is to encourage and support girls who are interested in STEM subjects.

Join us today to connect with female professionals in STEM and gain valuable mentorship and networking opportunities!

About Us

Empower Her is a non-profit organization that aims to support and empower girls and young women who are interested in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) fields. Our mission is to connect girls with successful women professionals in STEM and provide them with mentorship, guidance, and resources to help them achieve their goals and dreams.
